A week of guided Town & Country walks. Enjoy springtime along the coast or in Thanet's countryside. Join experienced leaders to try new routes and discover hidden places in this island corner of East Kent.


The Thanet Walking Festival has been held for ten years and brings together in one week the most popular of local walks.
